How Point Schemes Work

Formula:
Points = (Teams - Place + 1) × Point Scheme

Example with 4 teams, 2× multiplier:

  • 1st place: (4 - 1 + 1) × 2 = 8 points
  • 2nd place: (4 - 2 + 1) × 2 = 6 points
  • 3rd place: (4 - 3 + 1) × 2 = 4 points
  • 4th place: (4 - 4 + 1) × 2 = 2 points
